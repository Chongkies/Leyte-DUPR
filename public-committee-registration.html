📋 Committee Code of Integrity

I, the undersigned, hereby commit to the following principles as a certified Committee Member of the Leyte Pickleball League:

🎯 Unbiased Assessment: I will evaluate every player based strictly on the established USAP/DUPR criteria, regardless of personal affiliation, team loyalty, or hometown.
⚖️ Anti-Sandbagging Policy: I understand that intentionally under-rating a high-skill player is a violation of league trust. I will not manipulate scores to provide any player or team an unfair advantage in tournament brackets.
🔐 Professional Accountability: I accept that my name is permanently linked to every assessment I submit. If a pattern of inaccurate or dishonest rating is discovered, I will be held responsible and my Committee privileges will be revoked.
📊 Data Stewardship: I will treat player information with respect and ensure that the digital record remains a true reflection of our community's growth.
